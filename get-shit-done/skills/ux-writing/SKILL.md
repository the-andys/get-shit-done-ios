<essential_principles>
## How This Skill Works

1. **Clarity > Voice > Craft.** Precedence chain: if clarity and voice conflict, clarity wins. Craft refines after both are satisfied.
2. **PACE framework.** Every piece of interface text considers: Purpose, Anticipation, Context, Empathy.
3. **Voice is constant, tone adapts.** Voice is the product's personality (3-4 qualities). Tone dials those qualities up or down based on the situation.
4. **Write for the moment.** Consider what the user just did, what they need next, and their emotional state right now.
5. **Remove filler.** Every word must earn its place. Cut adverbs, interjections, and pleasantries unless they serve the voice.
</essential_principles>

<canonical_terminology>
## Terminology

- **voice** (not: brand language — voice is one aspect of brand)
- **tone** (not: mood — tone is the adaptation of voice to situation)
- **PACE** (Purpose, Anticipation, Context, Empathy)
- **interface text** (not: microcopy — that's reductive)
- **copy** (not: content — copy is specific to UI text)
</canonical_terminology>
